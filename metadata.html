Labour Market Dataset

Identifier link alla cartella ZIP
Title GenO - Labour Market
Description This dataset contains data from two different organization: ILOSTAT and The World Bank. The dataset is focused on gender equality in the labour market, in particular on the female's employment. For both datasets, data are divided by country and they cover almost all the existing states. The temporal coverages of the datasets are different: the data collected in the first dataset goes from 1983 to 2019, the data collected in the second dataset goes from 2014 to 2020.

Education Dataset

Identifier link alla cartella ZIP
Title GenO - Education
Description This dataset contains data from two different organization: the UNICEF Data Warehouse and the project “Our World in Data” of the Global Change Data Lab. The dataset is focused on educational attainment, divided by level of education, age and gender. In particular, the first dataset refers especially on the upper secondary education, the second dataset deals with primary education and gender inequality in education. For all datasets, data are divided by country and they cover almost all the existing states. The temporal coverages of the datasets are different: the data collected in the first dataset goes from 1970 to 2020, the data collected in the second dataset, for the first theme goes from 1970 to 2020, meanwhile the second theme goes from 1870 to 2010.
Modified 22-01-21
Theme Population and Society, Education, Culture and Sport.
Rights Holder UNESCO Institute for Statistics (UIS), Global Change Data Lab.
Accrual Periodicity Annual
Subject 32 education and communication, 3206 education , 280 access to education , 1419 elimination of illiteracy , 784 general education, 789 compulsory education, 3968 schooling.
